How to Navigate the Checker

Enter OpenEdu Number given to you by your school e.g. HOL-1234-123

General Requirements to Access the Checker

  • Full payment of any fees owed to the school
  • Index Number from current school
  • Internet Connection

About Us

OpenEdu-GH is a free-to-access portal for students and others willing to explore educational materials, tutorials and videos. All resources are provided by OpenEdu to support a new wave of dynamic learning. The Checker is a free web app sponsored by openEdu to enable Ghanaian students access end of term results in a simple, efficient and comfortable manner.
Our goal is to ensure students have their results stored in one place as a portfolio. This we believe will help to access any type of scores needed in the future such as applying for tertiary schools in and out of the country.
